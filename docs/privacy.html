Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

NtfyControl ("the App") is a remote-control tool that lets you monitor and manage your own Mac from your iPhone. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

1. Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information on our servers. The App has no backend infrastructure operated by us. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting services included.

2. Data stored on your device

  • Server URL & bearer token — stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain on your device. Never transmitted to any third party.
  • Cloudflare Access credentials — stored in the iOS Keychain if you use Cloudflare Zero Trust. Never transmitted to anyone other than your own Cloudflare tunnel.
  • APNs device token — sent once to your own Mac server (the server you set up and control) so it can deliver push alerts to your iPhone. Not shared with any third party.

3. Communication

All communication is between your iPhone and your own Mac server. The App connects only to the server URL you configure. No data passes through our servers because we do not operate any.

4. Push notifications

The App may receive push notifications delivered via Apple Push Notification service (APNs). These notifications are sent by your own Mac server using credentials you generate. Apple's handling of notification delivery is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

5. Third-party services

If you choose to expose your Mac server through Cloudflare Tunnel, your traffic passes through Cloudflare's network. Refer to Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
